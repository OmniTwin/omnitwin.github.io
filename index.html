The platform

You can't automate what you can't see.

Most enterprise work happens between the systems of record — in email, spreadsheets and the cross-app steps no dashboard can see. OmniTwin discovers how that work actually happens, helps you optimize it, automates what matters, and governs every AI action — in one architecture.

01Discover

See how work really happens

Designed to observe how work happens across SAP, Salesforce, Outlook and Excel — including the cross-app steps no dashboard captures. Privacy-first: on-device, no full-screen capture.

02Optimize

Find what's worth improving

Surfaces the repetitive, high-value workflows — and what they cost you — and benchmarks them, so you fix and automate the right work, not everything.

03Automate

Run it, and keep it running

Turns proven workflows into automations that start supervised and earn autonomy as they prove themselves — detecting failures, proposing fixes, and always letting a human pull one back.

04Govern

Trust every AI action

Prevention, not detection — each action is checked against policy and blocked before it runs, not flagged after the damage. Fail-closed, with a tamper-evident, independently verifiable audit trail built for EU AI Act traceability.
